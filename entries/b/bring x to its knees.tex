\mainentry{bring X to its knees} v.

[common] To present a machine, operating system, piece of software, or algorithm
with a load so extreme or \citeentry{pathological} that it grinds to a halt.
``To bring a MicroVAX to its knees, try twenty users running \citeentry{vi} --
or four running \citeentry{EMACS}.'' Compare \citeentry{hog}.

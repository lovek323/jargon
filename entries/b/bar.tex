\mainentry{bar} /bar/ n.

\begin{inparaenum}
    \item {[}very common] The second \citeentry{metasyntactic variable}, after
        \citeentry{foo} and before \citeentry{baz}. ``Suppose we have two
        functions: FOO and BAR. FOO calls BAR\ldots''
    \item Often appended to \citeentry{foo} to produce \citeentry{foobar}.
\end{inparaenum}

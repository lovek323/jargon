\mainentry{BUAG} // n.

[abbreviation, from alt.fan.warlord] Big Ugly ASCII Graphic. Pejorative term for
ugly \citeentry{ASCII art}, especially as found in \citeentry{sig block}s. For
some reason, mutations of the head of Bart Simpson are particularly common in
the least imaginative \citeentry{sig block}s. See \citeentry{warlording}.

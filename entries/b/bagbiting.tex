\mainentry{bagbiting} adj.

Having the quality of a \citeentry{bagbiter}. ``This bagbiting system won't let
me compute the factorial of a negative number.'' Compare \citeentry{losing},
\citeentry{cretinous}, \citeentry{bletcherous}, `barfucious' (under
\citeentry{barfulous}) and `chomping' (under \citeentry{chomp}).

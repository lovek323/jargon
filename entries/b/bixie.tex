\mainentry{bixie} /bik'see/ n.

Variant \citeentry{emoticon}s used on BIX (the BIX Information eXchange). The
most common (\citeentry{smiley}) bixie is $<$@\_@$>$, representing two cartoon
eyes and a mouth. These were originally invented in an SF fanzine called APA-L
and imported to BIX by one of the earliest users.

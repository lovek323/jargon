\mainentry{BWQ} /B-W-Q/ n.

[IBM: abbreviation, `Buzz Word Quotient'] The percentage of buzzwords in a
speech or document\opt{changes}{\footnote{Was `documents', but we can all agree
that `a \dots documents' makes no sense.}}. Usually roughly proportional to
\citeentry{bogosity}. See \citeentry{TLA}.

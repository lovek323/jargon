\mainentry{buglix} /buhg'liks/ n.

[uncommon] Pejorative term referring to \citeentry{DEC}'s ULTRIX operating
system in its earlier severely buggy versions. Still used to describe ULTRIX,
but without nearly so much venom. Compare \citeentry{AIDX}, \citeentry{HP-SUX},
\citeentry{Nominal Semidestructor}, \citeentry{Telerat}, \citeentry{sun-stools}.

\mainentry{AI koans} /A-I koh'anz/ pl.n.

A series of pastiches of Zen teaching riddles created by Danny Hillis at the MIT AI Lab around various major figures of the Lab's culture
(several are included under \citeappendix{Some AI Koans} in Appendix A). See also \citeentry{ha ha only serious}, \citeentry{mu}, and
\citeentry{hacker humor}.

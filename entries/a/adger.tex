\mainentry{adger} /aj'r/ vt.

[UCLA mutant of \citeentry{nadger}, poss. also from the middle name of an infamous \citeentry{tenured graduate student}] To make a bonehead
move with consequences that could have been foreseen with even slight mental effort. E.g. ``He started removing files and promptly adgered
the whole project''. Compare the \citeentry{dumbass attack}.
